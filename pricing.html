Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the price?

Our pricing includes complete website design, development, basic SEO setup, responsive design for all devices, and free support for the specified period. Hosting and domain registration are not included but we can help you set them up.

How long does it take to build my website?

Typically, a Basic Website takes 7-10 business days, while Business Websites take 2-3 weeks depending on the complexity and your responsiveness in providing content and feedback.

Do you offer hosting services?

While we don't provide hosting directly, we can recommend reliable hosting providers and help you set up your hosting account. We also offer maintenance plans that include hosting management.

Can I update my website myself?

Yes! Our Business package includes a basic Content Management System (CMS) that allows you to easily update text, images, and blog posts without technical knowledge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, though we can discuss flexible payment plans if needed.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer affordable monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Ask us about our maintenance packages when we discuss your project.
